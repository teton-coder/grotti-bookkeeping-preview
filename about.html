Grotti Bookkeeping and Payroll Services is owned and run by Lydia Grotti. As a sole proprietor, Lydia works directly with every client, which means your books are handled by the person you actually talk to, not passed off to a rotating support team.

The mission is simple: take the financial stress off small business owners so they can get back to running their business. Whether it's catching up on months of disorganized records or keeping a tight ship month to month, Grotti brings order, accuracy, and peace of mind.
